# Adding CFD Textbooks and Papers

To maximize the knowledge base, you can add CFD textbooks and research papers as PDFs.

## Recommended Textbooks (if you have them):

### Classic CFD Textbooks:
1. **"Computational Fluid Dynamics" by John D. Anderson**
2. **"Computational Methods for Fluid Dynamics" by Ferziger & Peric**
3. **"An Introduction to Computational Fluid Dynamics" by Versteeg & Malalasekera**
4. **"Numerical Heat Transfer and Fluid Flow" by Patankar**
5. **"Turbulent Flows" by Pope**
6. **"Computational Fluid Dynamics: The Basics with Applications" by Anderson**

### How to Add:
1. Place PDF files in the `cfd_gpt` directory
2. Run `python ingest.py` again
3. The system will automatically detect and ingest all PDFs
